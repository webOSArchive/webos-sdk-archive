MIXIN

enyo.ProxyObject

From enyo26/enyo/source/kernel/mixins/ProxyObject.js

Description

A mixin designed to abstract interaction of the enyo.Object.get() and enyo.Object.set() methods. It does not need to be applied to subkinds of enyo.Object.
