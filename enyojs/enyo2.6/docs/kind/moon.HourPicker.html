private scrollToValue : undefined
Default:

If the formatted new and old values are the same, skip animating by not passing the old value to IntegerPicker.scrollToValue.

If the hour is changed by more than 12 but the locale is using 12 hour formatting, this will not prevent a big scroll through all intermediate values (e.g. from 3pm to 2am) even though it only has to scroll 1 index. This can be seen most easily by selecting a time between 2 and 3 pm on day when DST springs forward and then changing the meridiem to AM.
