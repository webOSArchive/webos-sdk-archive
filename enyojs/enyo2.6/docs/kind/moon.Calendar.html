moon.Calendar

From moonstone/source/Calendar.js

Description

moon.Calendar is a control that displays a monthly calendar, with the month name at the top and a grid of days, grouped into rows by week, below.

The header buttons are used to navigate to the desired month; the desired day is selected by tapping on it.

{kind: 'moon.Calendar', content: 'Calendar Title'}
